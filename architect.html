Design Architect AI

Design Architect AI combines the AI High-Level Design (HLD) Tool and the AI Low-Level Design (LLD) Assistant to optimize system design. The HLD Tool automates the creation and visualization of high-level architectures, integrating with CAD tools for seamless diagramming. The LLD Assistant supports this by generating detailed component designs and providing AI-recommended logic and code snippets. This integrated suite streamlines the design process from concept to completion, enhancing both efficiency and accuracy.
